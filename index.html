Projects

Sentiment Prediction of IMDB Movie Reviews

In this project, I trained a dense neural network (DNN) to predict the sentiment of a movie review. The data for this project was comprised of ~50000 IMDB reviews. I built a vocabulary based on the training data and used it to convert the reviews into tensors, serving as input for the DNN. The final model acheived an accuracy rate of 76.13% and an AUC of 90.18%. Tags: NLP, word embedding, neural networks

Deciphering Trends in the Architectural Industry through Topic Modelling

This project aimed to uncover themes and trends in architecture articles using Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA). Analyzing 800+ articles spanning 2011-2023 from five Middle East architecture and interior design websites, the study identified 9 key themes: public and urban development, residential space, cultural public spaces, spatial elements and lighting, natural spaces, wellness, office and building designs, sustainability, global development, and hotel spaces. Findings revealed a shift from wellness and public and urban spaces as prevalent topics in 2019 to an increased focus on hotel spaces and sustainability themes in 2023. Tags: webscraping, topic modelling, NLP, lda

Assessment of the Happiness Index of UP Diliman Undergraduate Students using Multivariate Analysis

This study aimed to determine the factors affecting the happiness index of UP Diliman undergraduate students, utilizing convenience sampling with 159 respondents. Factor analysis revealed six key influencers: perspective, time management, sleep, degree program, environmental setting, and social relationships. Cluster analysis identified three clusters: College of Arts and Letters, College of Science, and College of Social Sciences and Philosophy. The findings emphasize the importance of creating a conducive campus environment for both psychological and physical well-being. Tags: pca, factor analysis, cluster analysis

Population Growth Prediction using Multiple Linear Regression

This research aimed to create a model for predicting population growth rate using different health and socio-economic indicators. The final regression model contains the following regressors: population median, Gross National Income (GNI) per capita, contraceptive prevalence rate, government expenditure on education (% of GDP), and percentage of the antiretroviral therapy coverage among people with HIV. The findings of this study can be used to highlight the importance of population growth rate in a country-level specification. Tags: regression, predictive analytics
